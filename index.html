What is FixIt POS?

FixIt POS is a modern, lightweight desktop application designed specifically for electronics repair shops. Whether you fix smartphones, laptops, or general electronics, FixIt streamlines your entire workflow from the moment a customer walks in to the moment they pay. It replaces messy paper tickets and complex spreadsheets with a clean, intuitive digital interface.

Powerful Features

Everything you need to run your shop efficiently.

Repair Tracking

Track active repairs, mark them as ready, and maintain a complete history of all jobs done.

Inventory Control

Real-time stock management. Parts are automatically deducted when assigned to a repair ticket.

AI Assistant

Ask the built-in AI questions about your revenue, stock levels, or business trends instantly.

Financial Analytics

Visualize your revenue, costs, and net profit. Track damaged goods losses and returns.

WhatsApp Alerts

Send automated "Device Ready" messages to customers with one click, including the final price.

Excel Import/Export

Seamlessly backup your data or migrate from other systems using .xlsx files.

What Does It Fix?

Common repair shop headaches, solved.

Problem: Lost Paper Tickets

Handwritten receipts get lost, faded, or misread, causing customer disputes and confusion.

Solution: Digital Database

Every repair is logged digitally, searchable by name, device, or phone number instantly.

Problem: Inventory Guesswork

Not knowing if you have an iPhone 11 screen in stock until you physically check the bin.

Solution: Auto-Deduction

Inventory counts update automatically as you add parts to repair tickets. Low stock alerts keep you prepared.

Problem: Unknown Profits

Revenue is easy to track, but calculating true profit after parts cost, returns, and damages is hard.

Solution: Automated P&L

FixIt calculates your COGS (Cost of Goods Sold), damaged losses, and returns to show your actual Net Profit.
